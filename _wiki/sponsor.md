## Where your support goes
{: .subsection-header.mt-5 }

Your sponsorship helps us to:

- Keep Arkime <strong>100% open source</strong> — no paid-only features, ever
- Fund development, bug fixes, and new capabilities
- Pay for the [demo](https://demo.arkime.com) systems, [CTF](https://ctf.arkime.com), and testing infrastructure
- Pay for our domains and Google Workspace
- Support community events like [Arkimeet](/arkimeet)

## Sponsorship FAQ
{: .subsection-header.mt-4 }

#### Which option should I choose?
{: .mt-3 }

If you have (or don't mind creating) a GitHub account, **GitHub Sponsors** is the best value — 0% fees means every cent reaches Arkime. If you'd rather not sign up for anything, **Buy Me a Coffee** lets you give in seconds with just a card.

#### Can I make a one-time donation instead of a monthly one?
{: .mt-3 }

Yes! Both options support one-time gifts as well as recurring support. Give whatever works for you.

#### Can I purchase support?
{: .mt-3 }

Yes. Our [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/awick) page has several tiers, some of which include different levels of support. If none of the tiers fit your needs, or you're looking for something custom, email [arkime@arkime.com](mailto:arkime@arkime.com) and we'll work out an arrangement that works for you.

#### Can my company sponsor another way?
{: .mt-3 }

Absolutely — for invoicing, wire transfer, or other arrangements, email [arkime@arkime.com](mailto:arkime@arkime.com) and we'll help.
