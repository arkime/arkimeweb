Sponsorship FAQ

Which option should I choose?

If you have (or don't mind creating) a GitHub account, GitHub Sponsors is the best value: 0% fees means every cent reaches Arkime. If you'd rather not sign up for anything, Buy Me a Coffee lets you give in seconds with just a card.

Can I make a one-time donation instead of a monthly one?

Yes! Both options support one-time gifts as well as recurring support. Give whatever works for you.

Can I purchase support?

Yes. Our GitHub Sponsors page has several tiers, some of which include different levels of support. If none of the tiers fit your needs, or you're looking for something custom, email arkime@arkime.com and we'll work out an arrangement that fits.

Can my company sponsor another way?

Absolutely. For invoicing, wire transfer, or other arrangements, email arkime@arkime.com and we'll help.
