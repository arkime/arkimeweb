Mini Arkimeetus

Calling all Arkimists! Join us twice a month for ☕ open office hours, a relaxed, informal space to connect with the Arkime developers and the wider Arkime community. Whether you're brand new to Arkime or a seasoned packet wrangler, you're welcome to drop in. Before each session we post a topic to Slack, but you're always free to bring your own questions or just hang out and chat.

Sessions are held on the first and third Wednesdays of each month at 2pm US/Eastern, hosted over Google Meet.

  • See you on the first and third Wednesdays of each month at 2pm US/Eastern.

Hope to see you there!
